# "Tasks not visible" — empty queue diagnosis (not a frontend bug)

## Symptom
Samarth: "the tasks are not visible, only create post is working." Queue showed
"All clear" while he expected LinkedIn commenting tasks.

## Root cause (data/timing, NOT code)
At the moment checked, SignalScope's Veloka feed had **0 fresh
`linkedin_engagement` tasks** — only 9 `top_x` ("Top 10 leads"), which the app
correctly hides (`FEATURES.otherCards: false`, Kunal Jun12 strip). So the queue
was legitimately empty.

Why no LinkedIn tasks:
- `linkedin_engagement` tasks are generated by the **LinkedIn-posts scan**
  (`POST /api/linkedin-posts`, RapidAPI) and written to the Tasks table.
- SignalScope's feed (`/api/sidekick/feed`) applies a **7-day post-freshness
  gate**: `POST_DATE_GATE` excludes any `linkedin_engagement` task whose
  `{Post Date}` is older than 7 days (aged-out tasks also get `{Archived At}`
  stamped). See `news-material/app/api/sidekick/feed/route.js` PENDING_FILTER.
- So the feed goes EMPTY whenever there's no recent scan batch. It refills when
  the scan runs (weekly Monday auto-scan cron, or a manual scan).

## Verification (frontend is correct)
- `/api/feed?limit=20` → 0 linkedin then, later 13 linkedin + 7 top_x (scan
  produced 16). Scoped count `/api/count?taskType=linkedin_engagement,unipile_`
  went 0 → 16.
- `orderedQueue` memo (SideKick.jsx ~1395): when `otherCards` off, stack =
  `unipileSignals + linkedin_engagement`. No inclusion filter drops fresh
  LinkedIn cards (freshnessBoost only re-orders). So the app renders them as
  soon as they exist. Confirmed: once 16 tasks landed, the app showed "16 left"
  + a LinkedIn Posts card (Comment on LinkedIn / Mark Done / Skip).

## Triage checklist for next "tasks not visible"
1. `curl .../api/feed?limit=30` → look at the `task_type` mix. Only `top_x`
   (or empty) = no fresh LinkedIn tasks; the app is behaving correctly.
2. `curl .../api/count?taskType=linkedin_engagement,unipile_` → 0 confirms it.
3. Fix = run/await the LinkedIn-posts scan (the weekly Veloka cron normally
   feeds it). NOT a frontend change. Do NOT flip `otherCards` (reintroduces
   the top_x/movement/GA cards Kunal deliberately stripped).
4. If the feed keeps drying up, check the auto-scan cron health (slack-agent
   container: `Veloka LinkedIn Posts — weekly auto-scan + resume`).
